Our Mission

Ar-T was born from a simple yet powerful idea: what if everyone could experience the world's greatest artworks in their own space? We believe art should be accessible to all, transcending physical and geographical limitations.

Through augmented reality, we're breaking down the barriers between people and art, making it possible to study, appreciate, and interact with masterpieces in ways never before possible.

Meet the Creator

Filippo Labate

As a passionate developer and art enthusiast, I created Ar-T to bridge the gap between traditional art appreciation and modern technology. With a background in XR development and a love for art history, I am dedicated to making art more accessible and interactive for everyone.
