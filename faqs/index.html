Frequently Asked Questions

Find Answers to Common Questions About Ar-T

General Questions

What is Ar-T?

Ar-T is an augmented reality application that allows you to experience famous artworks in your own space using Meta Quest devices. It combines art appreciation with cutting-edge AR technology.

Do I need a VR headset?

Yes, Ar-T currently requires a Meta Quest device (Quest 2, Quest 3, Quest Pro, or Quest 3S) to experience the AR features.

Is Ar-T free?

Yes, Ar-T is completely free to download and use on compatible Meta Quest devices.

Can I use Ar-T offline?

It is not possible to use Ar-T offline (yet).

How many artworks are available?

Ar-T currently features most of the artworks in the world. We regularly add new pieces to our collection. If you notice one missing, write to us at arteapp.info@gmail.com so that we can add it as soon as possible

Technical & Compatibility

How does AR placement work?

Ar-T uses advanced AR tracking to detect surfaces in your environment. You can then place artworks on these surfaces, and they will maintain their position and scale as you move around to provide a realistic viewing experience.

What devices are supported?

Ar-T is compatible with Meta Quest 2, Quest 3, Quest Pro, and Quest 3S devices. We're constantly evaluating support for additional platforms.

Does Ar-T work in all lighting conditions?

Ar-T works best in well-lit environments. While it can function in lower light, optimal AR tracking requires good lighting for the best experience.

How much space do I need?

You can use Ar-T in any space with a flat wall. Ar-T automatically places artworks in your available space, making it perfect for both small apartments and large rooms. If some artworks are bigger than the space avaiable, Ar-T will ignore physical boundaries and will show the entire artwork at its true size
